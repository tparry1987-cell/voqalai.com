Voqal AI vs Air AI.

Inbound AI receptionist vs outbound AI calling agent. Different tools for different jobs.

At a Glance.

Voqal AI is an inbound AI receptionist service — answering your business calls 24/7 with sector-specific training. Air AI is primarily an outbound AI calling agent designed for sales prospecting, lead follow-up, and appointment setting at scale. While both use AI voice technology, they serve fundamentally different purposes.

Detailed Analysis.

Core Purpose

Voqal AI answers your phone — inbound calls from patients, clients, and customers get handled with sector-specific intelligence, appointment booking, and caller routing. Air AI makes calls for you — outbound prospecting, lead nurture sequences, and sales appointment setting. They're complementary, not competing solutions.

Verdict: Different tools entirely. Voqal AI for inbound reception; Air AI for outbound sales automation.

Technology Approach

Voqal AI is built on Retell AI and Vapi — world-leading platforms for natural conversational voice AI, optimised for UK and US accents, terminology, and compliance. Air AI uses proprietary autonomous AI focused on scripted outbound conversations, designed to handle objections and book meetings.

Verdict: Voqal AI for natural inbound conversations; Air AI for structured outbound sales flows.

Pricing & Commitment

Voqal AI offers transparent pricing from £197/month, no contracts, cancel anytime. Air AI operates with custom enterprise pricing typically requiring annual commitments, costs not publicly listed, and generally higher price points targeting sales organisations with larger budgets.

Verdict: Voqal AI for accessible, transparent pricing; Air AI requires larger budget and longer commitment.

UK Market Suitability

Voqal AI is built for UK businesses with GDPR compliance, British terminology, UK accents, and understanding of NHS, SRA, CQC and other UK regulatory frameworks. Air AI is US-focused with limited UK market support and no UK-specific compliance features.

Verdict: Voqal AI for UK businesses; Air AI primarily for US sales teams.

Who Should Choose Which?

Choose Voqal AI if you:

  • Need to answer inbound business calls
  • Want sector-specific reception (dental, medical, legal, trades)
  • Operate in the UK or serve UK+US markets
  • Prefer transparent, no-contract pricing
  • Need 24/7 coverage for incoming calls
  • Want a managed service with expert setup

Choose Air AI if you:

  • Need to make outbound sales or prospecting calls at scale
  • Have a sales team wanting AI-assisted cold calling
  • Are a US-based sales organisation
  • Can commit to annual contracts and higher budgets
  • Want autonomous AI that handles sales objections
  • Need to scale outbound calling beyond human capacity

Final Recommendation.

For businesses receiving inbound calls: Voqal AI is the right choice — Air AI is not designed for inbound reception.

For sales teams wanting outbound automation: Air AI is purpose-built for outbound; Voqal AI is not an outbound tool.

For UK businesses: Voqal AI — Air AI has minimal UK presence and no UK compliance features.

For businesses wanting both: Use Voqal AI for inbound reception AND a separate outbound tool if needed.
