Voqal AI vs Smith.ai.

UK-focused AI-native vs US-focused hybrid. Which AI receptionist fits your market?

At a Glance.

Voqal AI is a Manchester-based AI-native receptionist service built on world-leading voice AI platforms (Retell AI, Vapi), delivering premium conversational quality for UK and US businesses across dental, medical, legal, and trade sectors. Smith.ai is a US-focused hybrid human+AI answering service with strong North American presence but limited UK coverage and US-centric pricing.

Detailed Analysis.

Geographic Focus

Voqal AI operates from Manchester, UK, serving both UK and US markets with services optimized for both regions' terminology (e.g., "surgery" vs "office" for medical practices) and compliance requirements (GDPR for UK, HIPAA awareness for US healthcare).

Smith.ai is headquartered in the US with services optimized for North American businesses. Offers UK coverage but receptionists and AI are trained primarily on US terminology, business practices, and time zones. UK clients may experience cultural mismatches.

Verdict: Voqal AI serves both UK and US businesses with regional optimization; Smith.ai focuses primarily on US market.

Service Model

Voqal AI is AI-only. Every call handled by voice AI with sector-specific training. No human receptionist involvement. Scales infinitely without staffing constraints.

Smith.ai combines AI with live receptionists. AI handles after-hours and routine calls; US-based human receptionists take complex inquiries, overflow, or when clients prefer human interaction. Also offers live chat for website visitors.

Verdict: Voqal AI for pure automation; Smith.ai for businesses wanting US-based human backup and live chat.

Pricing & Currency

Voqal AI uses custom pricing quoted in GBP after consultation. Based on call volume and sector requirements. No setup fees, no contracts, cancel anytime. Pricing structure opaque but flexible.

Smith.ai offers transparent tiered pricing in USD: approximately $90-300/month depending on call volume (20-200+ calls/month). No contracts. Additional charges for overflow calls. UK businesses deal with USD conversion and potential international payment fees.

Verdict: Smith.ai for pricing transparency; Voqal AI for UK currency and custom quotes. Budget certainty favors Smith.ai despite currency conversion.

Call & Chat Coverage

Voqal AI handles phone calls only. No live chat, email support, or multi-channel communication. Focused exclusively on voice AI.

Smith.ai offers both phone answering and live website chat. Single platform for multiple communication channels. Useful for businesses wanting unified reception across phone and web.

Verdict: Smith.ai for multi-channel coverage; Voqal AI for phone-only specialization.

Sector Expertise

Voqal AI specializes in sectors across UK and US markets: dental practices, medical offices, legal firms, estate agents/real estate, tradespeople (plumbers, electricians), accountants, and veterinary clinics. Pre-trained on regional terminology, compliance (GDPR for UK, relevant US regulations), and sector-specific workflows.

Smith.ai serves general business clients without sector specialization. Receptionists trained on individual client scripts but not UK-specific professional terminology or compliance at scale.

Verdict: Voqal AI for sector-specific needs in UK or US markets; Smith.ai for general US business reception.

Who Should Choose Which?

Choose Voqal AI if you:

  • Are a UK or US business needing sector-specific AI
  • Want world-class AI voice technology (Retell AI, Vapi)
  • Operate in dental, medical, legal, or trade sectors
  • Want regional terminology optimization and compliance
  • Prefer pure AI automation without human involvement
  • Need flexible pricing in GBP or USD
  • Value natural-sounding, human-like conversations

Choose Smith.ai if you:

  • Are a US business or serve primarily US clients
  • Want both phone and live chat coverage
  • Need US-based human receptionist backup
  • Prefer transparent tiered pricing despite USD conversion
  • Operate in legal or professional services (Smith.ai's specialty)
  • Value established US market reputation
  • Want human escalation for complex situations

Final Recommendation.

For UK businesses: Voqal AI offers world-leading voice technology, local terminology, GDPR compliance, GBP pricing, and sector specialization. No contracts reduce risk.

For US businesses needing pure AI: Voqal AI delivers premium voice technology with sector-specific training. Smith.ai provides hybrid human+AI if you need live backup.

For businesses needing live chat: Smith.ai if multi-channel is non-negotiable. Otherwise, use Voqal AI for phone + separate chat tool.

For premium voice quality: Voqal AI's world-leading technology platforms (Retell AI, Vapi) deliver superior conversational AI compared to Smith.ai's proprietary system.
