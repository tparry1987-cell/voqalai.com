## Lead Reactivation — Pay per result

- £0 upfront
- Client provides dormant lead database (200+ contacts minimum)
- AI contacts leads within 48 hours of handover
- Client pays only when a lead converts (booked appointment, completed form, qualified call)
- No contracts, no lock-ins
- Per-conversion price set during discovery call (depends on average customer value and target ROI)
- Typical reactivation rate: 15-20% (vs 2-5% manual follow-up)
