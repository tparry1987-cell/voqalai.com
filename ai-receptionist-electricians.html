When you’re halfway through a consumer unit upgrade, rewiring a property, or installing an EV charger, answering your phone isn’t an option. Every unanswered call is a potential job walking straight to a competitor. Voicemail doesn’t cut it. Customers with power outages, tripped circuits, or sparking sockets aren’t going to wait. They need someone to pick up, understand the problem, and confirm you’re on it. That’s exactly what our AI receptionist does.
