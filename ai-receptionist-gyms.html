When your staff are coaching sessions, managing the gym floor, or dealing with members face-to-face, the phone goes unanswered. For gyms and fitness studios, the busiest call times — early mornings, lunchtimes, and evenings — are exactly when your team is busiest on the floor. Potential members who can’t get through don’t leave voicemails. They search for the next gym down the road. Your AI receptionist makes sure every call is answered, every enquiry is captured, and every prospect gets a professional response.
