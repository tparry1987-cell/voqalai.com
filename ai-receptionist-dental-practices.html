What Is an AI Receptionist for Dental Practices?

An AI receptionist for dental practices is a voice-powered virtual assistant that answers patient calls 24/7, books and manages appointments, handles NHS and private enquiries, and triages dental emergencies — all without requiring a human to pick up the phone. It integrates with practice management systems like Dentally and Software of Excellence to check real-time availability.
