Service Overview.

Voqal AI provides AI voice agents designed to replace or supplement human receptionists for small and medium-sized UK businesses. The service uses conversational AI trained on sector-specific terminology and workflows, enabling natural-sounding call handling for appointment scheduling, customer inquiries, and call routing.

Technology Foundation

Voqal AI is built on the world's leading voice AI platforms — Retell AI and Vapi — ensuring best-in-class conversational quality, natural-sounding voices, and cutting-edge natural language processing. Serving businesses in both the UK and US, clients receive a dedicated phone number or can forward existing numbers to the AI receptionist.

Sector Specialization

The platform offers pre-trained models for:

  • Healthcare: Dental practices, GP surgeries, veterinary clinics
  • Professional Services: Legal firms, accountancy practices, estate agents
  • Trade Services: Plumbers, electricians, builders, handyman services
  • Business Services: Financial advisors, recruitment agencies, IT support

Market Position.

Voqal AI competes in the UK AI receptionist market alongside hybrid human+AI services (Moneypenny, AnswerConnect) and developer-focused platforms (Synthflow, Bland AI, Retell AI). The company differentiates through world-class voice technology, sector specialization, and flexible contracts.
