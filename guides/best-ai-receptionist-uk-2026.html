How to Choose an AI Receptionist.

1. Match to your sector

Healthcare, legal, and trade businesses need sector-specific compliance and terminology. Generic AI receptionists often struggle with specialist vocabulary and appointment protocols.

2. Prioritize appointment integration

If you use booking software (Calendly, Cal.com, Acuity), confirm native integration. Manual calendar coordination defeats the purpose of automation.

3. Test the voice quality

Call the demo line before committing. Natural conversation flow matters more than feature lists. Robotic voices damage your brand.

4. Check escalation paths

Understand what happens when AI can't handle a call. Some providers offer human backup, others transfer to your team, some just take messages.

5. Verify contract flexibility

Avoid 12-month lock-ins for your first AI receptionist. Usage patterns are unpredictable in the first 90 days, and switching costs are low in this market.
