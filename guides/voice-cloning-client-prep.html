Voice Cloning Preparation Guide

Everything you need to record the perfect voice sample for your AI agent

1 What We Need From You

We'll use a short recording of your voice to create an AI voice agent that sounds just like you. Your clients will hear your voice when they call, 24/7 - even when you're on a shoot.

Recording Length
3 - 5 minutes
File Format
MP3 or WAV
Environment
Quiet room, no echo
Equipment
Phone voice memo is fine

Why 3-5 minutes?

The AI needs enough audio to learn the unique characteristics of your voice - your tone, pace, accent, and natural inflections. More audio = a more accurate clone. Aim for 5 minutes if possible.

2 Recording Tips

Follow these tips to get the best possible voice clone:

  1. Record in the quietest room available - a carpeted room or even a wardrobe/closet is ideal
  2. Hold your phone or microphone 6-8 inches from your mouth
  3. Use your natural, friendly phone voice - not a "reading aloud" voice
  4. Keep your pace consistent and conversational - don't rush
  5. If you make a mistake, pause for 2 seconds then re-read the sentence
  6. No background music, TV, or other noise
  7. Avoid rooms with hard surfaces (tiles, glass) as they create echo
  8. Only your voice should be in the recording - no other speakers

Section 5 - Questions & Wrap-up

Absolutely, no problem at all. I completely understand - it's a big decision, and you want to make sure you find the right photographer for your special day.

Is there anything else I can help you with today? Perfect. Well, it's been lovely chatting with you. I'll follow up with an email shortly, and please don't hesitate to reach out if you have any more questions. Take care, and I look forward to working with you!
