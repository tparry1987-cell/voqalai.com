# Voice Cloning - Recording Preparation Guide

## What We Need From You

A **1-2 minute audio recording** of you reading the script below. This will be used to create your AI voice agent that sounds just like you.

**Important:** Keep it to 1-2 minutes. Longer recordings (over 3 minutes) actually reduce quality.

---

## Recording Requirements

- **Length:** 1-2 minutes (do NOT record more than 3 minutes)
- **Format:** MP3 or WAV (voice memo on your phone works)
- **Environment:** Quiet room, no background noise, no echo

## Recording Tips

1. Record in the quietest room available (carpeted room or closet is ideal)
2. Hold your phone/mic about 20cm (two fists' distance) from your mouth, at a slight angle
3. Use your natural, friendly phone voice (not a "reading" voice)
4. Keep your pace consistent and conversational (don't rush or slow down)
5. Stick to ONE style throughout - don't switch between energetic and calm
6. If you make a mistake, pause for 2 seconds and re-read the sentence
7. No background music, TV, fans, air conditioning, or other noise
8. Avoid rooms with hard surfaces (they create echo)
9. Only your voice should be in the recording - no other speakers
10. Avoid "ums," "ahs," and long pauses - the AI will clone those too
11. Turn off your phone notifications before recording

## The Script

Read this naturally, as if you're chatting with a client on the phone. Aim for about 1-2 minutes total.

---

### Section 1 - Greeting & Services

Hi there, thanks so much for calling! My name is [YOUR NAME], and I'm really glad you reached out. Whether you're looking for wedding photography, family portraits, or something completely unique, I'd love to help you capture those special moments.

I offer a wide range of photography services. Weddings are probably my biggest passion - there's nothing quite like capturing that first look, the laughter during the speeches, and all those beautiful candid moments throughout the day. But I also love doing family sessions, corporate headshots, engagement shoots, and special events.

### Section 2 - Booking & Details

Great question! Let me check my availability for you. I'd recommend booking as early as possible, especially for weekend dates during the summer months. Those tend to fill up quite quickly.

Once we've agreed on a date, I'll send over a booking form and a contract for you to review. There's a deposit required to secure your date, and then the remaining balance is due two weeks before the event.

### Section 3 - Wrap-up

Would you like me to email you my full price list? I can also send over some examples from recent shoots so you can get a feel for my style.

Is there anything else I can help you with today? Perfect. Well, it's been lovely chatting with you. I'll follow up with an email shortly, and please don't hesitate to reach out if you have any more questions. Take care, and I look forward to working with you!
