Guide

How to set up an AI receptionist for your UK business.

Last updated: 7 April 2026

A step-by-step walkthrough from choosing your plan to taking your first live call. No technical skills required — go live in 48 hours with zero setup fees.

Before you start.

Setting up an AI receptionist with Voqal AI is straightforward. You will need your business information (name, opening hours, services), a list of common caller questions, and your existing business phone number. If you want automatic appointment booking, have your calendar login details ready.

The entire process takes around 48 hours from sign-up to live calls. There are no setup fees on any plan.

Step 1

Choose your plan.

Visit the pricing page and select the plan that matches your call volume and integration needs.

No contracts on any plan. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time.

Step 2

Share your business information.

You provide the details that train your AI receptionist. This is what makes the agent sound like it genuinely knows your business rather than reading from a generic script.

What you will need to share:

  • • Business name and address
  • • Opening hours (including any variations for weekends or holidays)
  • • Services offered and pricing
  • • Common questions callers ask (FAQs)
  • • Any specific terminology used in your industry
  • • Staff names and roles (for call transfers)

The more context you provide, the more natural and accurate the AI sounds. We build a comprehensive knowledge base tailored to your sector — dental, medical, legal, property, trades, or any other industry.

Step 3

Define your call handling rules.

You decide how the AI handles different types of calls. This is where you configure the logic that determines the caller experience.

Common configurations:

  • • During hours: AI answers, books appointments, transfers urgent calls to staff
  • • After hours: AI answers, takes messages, books next-day appointments
  • • Overflow: AI picks up when your team is busy (e.g., after 3 rings)
  • • Emergency: Immediate transfer to a specific staff member or on-call number
Step 4

Connect your phone number.

You have two options: forward your existing business number to your dedicated Voqal AI number, or receive a new UK number from the platform.

Call forwarding is configured through your phone provider (BT, Virgin, Vodafone, etc.) and typically takes under 10 minutes. We provide the exact forwarding instructions for your specific provider.

If you prefer a new number, we provision a UK landline or mobile number that is ready to use immediately.

Step 5

Integrate your calendar and CRM.

Connect your scheduling and customer management tools so the AI can book appointments and log calls automatically. This step is optional on the Starter plan and included on Pro and Premium.

Supported integrations:

• Google Calendar

• HubSpot

• Microsoft Outlook

• Salesforce

• Calendly

• Zoho CRM

• Cal.com

• Pipedrive

Industry-specific tools (Dentally, Cliniko, Clio, Xero) are also supported. Don't see your tool? Zapier connects Voqal AI to 6,000+ applications.

Step 6

Test your AI receptionist.

Before going live, you make test calls to verify everything works as expected. This is your opportunity to fine-tune the experience.

What to test:

  • • General enquiry handling — does the AI answer common questions accurately?
  • • Appointment booking — does it check availability and confirm correctly?
  • • Call transfers — does it route urgent calls to the right person?
  • • After-hours behaviour — does it follow your out-of-hours rules?
  • • Message taking — do staff receive notifications correctly?

Review call transcripts in your dashboard after each test. Adjustments to scripts, tone, or routing can be made at any time.

Step 7

Go live and monitor.

Once you are satisfied with testing, activate your AI receptionist. It begins handling real calls immediately — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Your dashboard shows:

  • • Live call log with full transcripts
  • • Appointments booked and confirmed
  • • Messages taken and delivered
  • • Call duration and minute usage
  • • Caller sentiment and outcomes

Staff receive email and SMS notifications for every call. You can adjust your AI receptionist's behaviour at any time — no need to wait for a support ticket.
