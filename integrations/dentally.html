Integration

Voqal AI + Dentally.

Connect your Dentally practice management system to Voqal AI. Every patient call answered, every appointment booked into the right treatment room, every clinician schedule respected — without your reception team lifting a finger.

How the integration works.

Voqal AI connects to Dentally through a secure API link. Once authorised, the AI receptionist gains read and write access to your patient records, appointment diary, clinician schedules, and treatment room availability.

When a patient calls, Voqal AI matches them against your Dentally database using their name, date of birth, or phone number. Existing patients are recognised instantly. New patients are registered in Dentally during the call, with all relevant details captured before the appointment is confirmed.

The integration handles the complexity of dental scheduling — NHS band allocations, private treatment pricing tiers, hygienist versus dentist availability, and multi-surgery room management — so your front desk can focus on the patients already in the practice.

What syncs.

Patient Record Matching

Voqal AI searches Dentally in real time to identify returning patients by name, date of birth, or contact number. Verified patients are linked to their existing record — no duplicate entries, no manual look-ups.

Treatment Room & Clinician Booking

The AI checks clinician diaries and treatment room allocations within Dentally before offering appointment slots. It respects individual clinician working patterns, lunch breaks, and room assignments across your practice.

NHS vs Private Appointment Routing

Voqal AI identifies whether the caller requires an NHS or private appointment and books the correct type in Dentally. NHS band rules, exemption categories, and private fee structures are all handled during the call.

Recall & Follow-Up Automation

Patients overdue for check-ups or hygiene visits are flagged automatically. When they call in, Voqal AI prompts rebooking based on their recall interval stored in Dentally, keeping your appointment book consistently full.

Built for dental practices.

The Dentally integration is designed for how dental practices actually operate. Whether you run a single surgery or a multi-site group, Voqal AI adapts to your workflows. See our dedicated AI receptionist for dental practices page for the full picture.

  • Single-site dental practices — replace missed calls during busy morning slots when reception is overwhelmed with check-ins and phone lines ring simultaneously.
  • Multi-surgery dental groups — route patients to the correct location and clinician across multiple Dentally-connected sites, balancing appointment load automatically.
  • NHS and mixed practices — distinguish between NHS and private callers, apply the correct appointment types, and maintain accurate UDA tracking within Dentally.
  • Practices with high recall volumes — capture rebooking opportunities every time a recall patient calls in, reducing the backlog your reception team needs to chase manually.
  • Emergency dental slots — prioritise urgent cases like pain, swelling, or trauma into designated emergency appointment slots without disrupting the regular diary.
