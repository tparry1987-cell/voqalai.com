Integration

Voqal AI + Zapier.

Connect your AI receptionist to 6,000+ applications. Every phone call can trigger workflows, create records, and send notifications across your entire tool stack — without writing a single line of code.

How it works.

Zapier acts as a bridge between Voqal AI and thousands of other applications. When something happens in Voqal AI — a call is completed, a lead is captured, an appointment is booked — Zapier can automatically trigger actions in any connected app.

You build these connections visually using Zapier's drag-and-drop editor. No developers needed. A "Zap" consists of a trigger (the event in Voqal AI) and one or more actions (what happens next in your other tools). Multi-step Zaps chain together complex workflows from a single phone call.

This means if your CRM, project management tool, or notification system isn't on our native integrations list, you can still connect it through Zapier in minutes.

Capabilities

What you can automate.

Call-Triggered Workflows

Every completed call fires a Zapier trigger with full metadata: caller name, phone number, transcript, duration, outcome, and any data collected during the conversation. Use this to kick off downstream actions anywhere.

Multi-App Record Creation

Create records simultaneously across multiple tools from a single call. Add a contact to your CRM, create a task in your project manager, log a row in a spreadsheet, and send a team notification — all from one Zap.

Conditional Routing

Use Zapier's built-in filters to route calls based on any field. New leads go to your CRM. Support requests go to your helpdesk. Appointments trigger calendar events. Missed calls send urgent Slack alerts. Each path runs independently.

Real-Time Notifications

Get instant notifications in Slack, Microsoft Teams, email, or SMS when specific call events occur. Set up alerts for high-value leads, missed calls, after-hours enquiries, or any custom criteria that matters to your business.

Setup

Live in minutes.

1

Connect Voqal AI

Search for "Voqal AI" in Zapier's app directory and authorise the connection. Your API key links Zapier to your Voqal AI account securely.

2

Build Your Zap

Choose a Voqal AI trigger event, then add one or more actions from any of Zapier's 6,000+ supported apps. Use filters to control when the Zap fires.

3

Activate

Test with a sample call, verify the data flows correctly, and turn your Zap on. Workflows run automatically from that point forward — no ongoing maintenance required.
