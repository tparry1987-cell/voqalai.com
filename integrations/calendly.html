Integration

Voqal AI + Calendly.

Your AI receptionist checks Calendly availability in real time and books appointments while the caller is still on the phone. No hold music, no call-backs, no missed opportunities.

How the connection works.

When a caller asks to book an appointment, Voqal AI queries your Calendly account through a secure API connection. It reads your event types, checks which slots are genuinely free, and offers the caller a choice of times that suit them.

Once the caller picks a slot, the booking is confirmed in Calendly instantly. The caller receives an email confirmation, your calendar updates, and any connected integrations (Zoom, Google Meet, Teams) generate their meeting links automatically.

The entire process happens during the conversation. By the time the caller hangs up, the appointment is locked in and both parties have received confirmation.

What syncs.

Real-Time Availability

Voqal AI pulls live availability from your Calendly account, respecting working hours, buffer times, and minimum notice periods. Callers only hear slots that are genuinely open.

In-Call Booking

Appointments are created in Calendly the moment the caller confirms. No queues, no pending states — the slot is reserved immediately so double-bookings never happen.

Automatic Confirmations

Calendly sends its standard confirmation and reminder emails to both the caller and your team. Any connected video conferencing tools generate meeting links automatically.

Reschedule & Cancellation Handling

Returning callers can reschedule or cancel existing bookings during a phone call. Voqal AI finds their appointment, processes the change, and triggers updated notifications.
