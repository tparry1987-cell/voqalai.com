Integration

Voqal AI + Clio.

Connect your Clio Manage account to Voqal AI. Every client call answered, intake details captured, matters created, and call notes logged against the correct case file — while your solicitors focus on billable work.

How the integration works.

Voqal AI connects to Clio Manage through OAuth authorisation. Once your firm approves the connection, the AI receptionist gains secure access to your contacts, matters, calendar, and activity log — enabling it to handle calls with the same context your reception team would have.

When a client calls, Voqal AI searches Clio for their contact record using name, phone number, or email. Existing clients are matched to their file and the call is logged against their open matter. New enquiries trigger the intake workflow — the AI captures the caller's details, the nature of their legal issue, urgency, and preferred solicitor, then creates a draft contact and matter in Clio for your team to review.

Every call generates a structured activity entry in Clio: transcript, summary, outcome, and any follow-up actions. Your fee earners see a complete record of every client interaction without needing to take notes themselves.

What syncs.

Automated Client Intake

Voqal AI runs your intake questionnaire during the call — capturing the caller's legal issue, timeline, budget expectations, and contact preferences. All data is pushed into Clio as a new contact and draft matter, ready for your intake team to review.

Matter Creation & Tagging

New matters are created in Clio with the correct practice area, responsible solicitor, and originating solicitor pre-assigned. Custom fields and tags are applied based on the call content, so matters are categorised correctly from the outset.

Call Logging by Case File

Every call is logged as a communication activity against the relevant matter in Clio. The entry includes the full transcript, a concise summary, call duration, and any action items identified during the conversation.

Consultation Scheduling

Voqal AI checks solicitor availability in Clio's calendar and books initial consultations directly. The appointment appears in the solicitor's diary with the client details and matter reference already attached.

Live in three steps.

Built for law firms.

The Clio integration is designed around how UK law firms actually operate — from SRA compliance requirements to the realities of high-volume intake. See our dedicated AI receptionist for law firms page for the complete picture of how Voqal AI serves the legal sector.

  • High-street law firms — capture every new enquiry that calls during office hours (and after hours) without hiring additional reception staff, converting more calls into paying clients.
  • Personal injury practices — run structured intake during the initial call, capturing accident details, injury type, liability indicators, and limitation dates before creating the matter in Clio.
  • Conveyancing firms — handle high call volumes during exchange periods without calls going to voicemail, logging every conversation against the correct property transaction matter.
  • Family law practices — manage sensitive client calls with appropriate tone and confidentiality, booking initial consultations and capturing case details for divorce, child arrangement, and financial remedy matters.
  • Commercial law firms — route calls to the correct department, log enquiries against existing corporate matters, and book consultation slots with the relevant partner or associate.
