Integration

Voqal AI + HubSpot.

Every inbound call becomes a CRM record. Voqal AI connects directly to HubSpot so contacts are created, transcripts are logged, and deals are updated — all without your team lifting a finger.

How the connection works.

Voqal AI uses the HubSpot API to push data from every phone conversation into your CRM in real time. When a call ends, the integration checks whether the caller already exists as a contact. If they do, the record is updated with the latest call details. If they don't, a new contact is created automatically.

Call transcripts, outcomes, and custom data points are logged as activities on the contact timeline. If the call qualifies as a sales opportunity, a deal can be created or moved to the next pipeline stage — all based on rules you define.

The integration works with HubSpot Free, Starter, Professional, and Enterprise. No middleware, no Zapier, no manual CSV imports. Just clean data flowing straight into HubSpot.

What Syncs

Data that flows automatically.

Contact Auto-Creation

New callers are added to HubSpot as contacts with their name, phone number, email, and any details captured during the conversation. Existing contacts are matched and updated — no duplicates.

Call Transcript Logging

A full transcript of every call is attached to the contact record as a logged activity. Your team can review exactly what was said, search transcripts, and use them for training or compliance.

Deal Pipeline Sync

When a call qualifies as a sales opportunity, Voqal AI creates a deal in the pipeline you specify. Deal stage, value, and close date can all be set based on the conversation outcome.

Lead Scoring & Tagging

Contacts are tagged with lead source, call intent, and enquiry type. These properties feed into HubSpot lead scoring, so your sales team always knows which leads to prioritise.

Setup

Live in three steps.

1

Connect Your HubSpot API Key

Generate a private app token in HubSpot and paste it into your Voqal AI dashboard. The connection is authenticated and encrypted end-to-end.

2

Map Contact Properties

Choose which HubSpot properties receive data from calls. Map caller name, email, phone, enquiry type, and any custom fields you need.

3

Activate and Go Live

Switch the integration on and take a test call. Verify the data appears in HubSpot, then let it run. Average setup time: under 10 minutes.
