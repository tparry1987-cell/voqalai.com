Integration

Voqal AI + Google Calendar.

Your AI receptionist reads your Google Calendar in real time, finds open slots, and books appointments while the caller is on the line. Calendar invites go out automatically.

How the connection works.

Voqal AI connects to your Google account through a secure OAuth authorisation. It reads free/busy data across every calendar you select — your primary calendar, team calendars, and any shared calendars you have access to.

When a caller asks to book, the AI receptionist queries your Google Calendar for available time slots. It factors in existing events, all-day blocks, and out-of-office entries to ensure it only offers genuinely free times. The caller picks a slot, and a calendar event is created instantly with both parties added as attendees.

Google Calendar handles the rest: invitation emails are sent, reminders trigger at your configured intervals, and any connected video conferencing (Google Meet) generates a meeting link automatically.

What syncs.

Live Availability Reading

Voqal AI checks your Google Calendar in real time before offering any slots. It reads events across multiple calendars simultaneously, so double-bookings are impossible even if you manage several diaries.

Appointment Creation

Confirmed bookings appear on your Google Calendar within seconds. Events include the caller's name, phone number, email, and a summary of what the appointment is about — all captured during the conversation.

Multi-Calendar Support

Select which calendars to check for conflicts and which calendar to create new appointments on. Ideal for businesses where personal commitments and work appointments live on separate calendars.

Reminder & Notification Sync

Google Calendar sends invitation emails to the caller and your team automatically. Default reminders — email, pop-up, or SMS — fire at your configured intervals so nobody forgets their appointment.

Live in three steps.

1

Authorise Your Google Account

Click the connect button in the Voqal AI dashboard and sign in with your Google account. For Google Workspace accounts, your admin may need to approve the connection first.

2

Select Calendars to Check

Choose which calendars Voqal AI should read for availability and which calendar new appointments should be created on. You can select as many or as few as you need.

3

Enable AI Booking

Set your bookable hours, appointment durations, and any buffer time between meetings. Switch on the integration and your AI receptionist starts booking into your Google Calendar immediately.

Perfect for Google users.

If your business runs on Google, this integration means zero extra software for scheduling. Common use cases include:

  • Any business using Google Workspace that wants AI-powered phone booking without additional tools
  • Small practices and sole traders without dedicated scheduling software
  • Teams with shared Google calendars coordinating appointments across multiple staff members
  • Sole traders managing their own diary who want calls to book directly into their calendar
  • Businesses already using Google Meet that want meeting links generated automatically for phone-booked appointments
