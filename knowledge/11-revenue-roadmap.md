## Phased Roadmap

### Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-4) — Target: £0-997

**Goal:** First paying client

**Activities:**
- Launch industry-specific Instantly campaigns across all Zoho domains
- Publish waiting blog post drafts (SEO starts compounding)
- Offer lead reactivation as free trial to 3-5 businesses (build proof)
- Daily LinkedIn content (Tom's genuine voice)
- Book 5-10 demo calls via cold email + LinkedIn combined

**Key metric:** Demo calls booked per week
**Milestone:** First signed client (likely B&H Lite £997 or lead reactivation)

---

### Phase 2: First Revenue (Weeks 5-8) — Target: £500-1,500/mo

**Goal:** 2-3 paying clients, first real case study

**Activities:**
- Close 1-2 more clients from Phase 1 pipeline
- Convert lead reactivation trial clients to paid
- Build first genuine case study from client results
- Refine pitch based on demo call objections
- Add case study to website, cold emails, and LinkedIn
- Start targeting referral partners (accountants, marketing agencies serving SMEs)

**Key metric:** Close rate on demo calls
**Milestone:** First monthly recurring client (MRR > £0)

---

### Phase 3: Growth Engine (Months 3-4) — Target: £1,500-2,500/mo

**Goal:** 4-6 recurring clients, predictable pipeline

**Activities:**
- Scale cold email volume (all domains rotating, industry-specific sequences)
- Launch referral programme (existing clients refer → discount or commission)
- Create industry-specific landing pages with real case study data
- Explore Google Ads for high-intent keywords ("AI receptionist UK")
- Systematise onboarding (template flows, standard integrations) to reduce delivery time
- Consider hiring a VA for lead list building (free up Tom's time for sales calls)

**Key metric:** MRR growth rate month-over-month
**Milestone:** MRR hits £1,500+ with 4+ recurring clients

---

### Phase 4: Quit-Ready (Months 5-6) — Target: £2,750+/mo

**Goal:** Sustainable revenue to leave the day job

**Activities:**
- MRR should be £2,000+ from 5-8 managed clients
- B&H projects adding £500-1,000/month averaged
- Lead reactivation pipeline generating warm leads that convert to managed
- Pipeline has 3+ months visibility (enough leads in various stages)
- Save 3-month cash reserve (£6,000-8,000 buffer)
- Hand in notice at HR consultancy

**Key metric:** 3 consecutive months at £2,750+ revenue
**Milestone:** Tom goes full-time on Voqal AI

---

### Phase 5: Scale (Months 7-12) — Target: £5,000+/mo

**Goal:** Grow beyond solo founder

**Activities:**
- Full-time: 8+ hours/day on Voqal AI
- Target: 12-15 managed clients
- Consider first hire (delivery/support)
- Expand US market more aggressively
- Develop productised offerings (self-serve onboarding)
- Explore partnerships with CRM/scheduling platforms

**Key metric:** Revenue per hour of Tom's time
**Milestone:** First hire, MRR £4,000+
